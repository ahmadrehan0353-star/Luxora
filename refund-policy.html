Refund & Returns Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Return window

You may return most unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange.

Eligibility

  • Item must be unworn, unwashed and undamaged, with all original tags attached.
  • Item must be returned in its original packaging where possible.
  • Final-sale and clearance items are not eligible for return unless faulty.
  • Innerwear, swimwear and accessories may be excluded from returns for hygiene reasons.

How to start a return

  1. Sign in and go to My Account → Orders, or contact us with your order number.
  2. Let us know which item(s) you'd like to return and why.
  3. We'll arrange a pickup or provide return instructions.
  4. Once received and inspected, we'll process your refund or exchange.

Refunds

Since orders are paid via Cash on Delivery, approved refunds are issued via bank transfer or mobile wallet (e.g. JazzCash/Easypaisa) to the details you provide. Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 business days of us receiving the returned item. edit me — confirm your actual refund methods

Exchanges

Need a different size or colour? Let us know when you start your return and we'll arrange an exchange, subject to stock availability.

Damaged or incorrect items

If an item arrives damaged, defective, or different from what you ordered, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos, and we'll arrange a free replacement or full refund.

Questions

Reach out any time via our contact page and we'll be glad to help.
