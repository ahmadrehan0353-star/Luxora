Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Coverage

We currently ship nationwide across Pakistan. edit me — list specific cities/regions if coverage is limited

Delivery times

  • Major cities (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi): 2–4 business days
  • Other cities: 3–7 business days
  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days before dispatch.

Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to courier delays, weather, or high-demand periods such as sales.

Shipping charges

  • Free shipping on orders over Rs 5,000.
  • A flat shipping fee applies to orders below this amount, shown at checkout before you confirm.

Cash on Delivery

All orders are paid via Cash on Delivery. Please have the exact order amount ready for our courier at the time of delivery.

Order tracking

Once your order ships, you can track its status anytime from My Account → Orders.

Failed or missed deliveries

If you're unavailable to receive your order, our courier will typically attempt delivery again or contact you to arrange a new time. Orders that remain undeliverable after multiple attempts may be returned to us.

Questions

For anything not covered here, please contact our support team.
