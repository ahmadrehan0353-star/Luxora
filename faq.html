Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, shipping and returns.

Ordering & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) only. You pay in cash when your order is delivered to your door — no online payment or card details required.
How do I place an order?
Add items to your bag, go to checkout, enter your delivery address, choose a shipping option, and confirm — your payment will be collected as cash on delivery.
Can I change or cancel my order?
Contact us as soon as possible after placing your order. If it hasn't shipped yet, we'll do our best to update or cancel it for you.

Shipping

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery typically takes 3–5 business days within Pakistan, depending on your city. See our Shipping Policy for full details.
Is shipping free?
Orders over Rs 5,000 ship free. Orders below that threshold have a flat shipping fee shown at checkout.
How do I track my order?
Sign in and visit My Account → Orders to see the live status of every order you've placed.

Returns & Exchanges

Can I return or exchange an item?
Yes — unworn items with original tags attached can be returned within 30 days of delivery. See our Refund Policy for the full process.
When will I get my refund?
Once we've received and inspected your return, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.

Sizing & Products

How do I find my size?
Each product page includes a size guide with measurements. If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for a relaxed fit.
Do you restock sold-out items?
Popular styles are restocked when possible. Check back on the product page, or contact us to ask about a specific item.
