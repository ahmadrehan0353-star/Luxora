Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This policy explains what information LUXORA ("we", "us") collects when you use our website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication).
  • Order information: shipping address, phone number, and items purchased, needed to fulfil Cash on Delivery orders.
  • Usage information: pages viewed, products browsed, and items saved to your cart or wishlist, to improve your shopping experience.

We do not collect or store card or banking details, since all orders are paid via Cash on Delivery.

How we use your information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To manage your account, wishlist and order history.
  • To respond to customer support requests.
  • To send order updates and, if you opt in, newsletter emails about new arrivals and offers.

How we store your information

Account and order data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore and Storage), which applies industry-standard security practices. Access to the admin dashboard is restricted to authorised store administrators.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share order details only with the courier service used to deliver your order, and with service providers (such as Firebase) that help us operate the store.

Your choices

  • You can view and update your account details from My Account.
  • You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in those emails.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Cookies

We use local browser storage to remember items in your cart and wishlist between visits. This data stays on your device unless you're signed in, in which case it's linked to your account.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Get in touch and we'll be happy to help.
